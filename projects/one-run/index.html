One Run, One Philippines: Isang Bayan para sa Kalikasan

Is a simultaneous eco-run in Quezon City, Cebu, Bacolod, Davao and Los Angeles that calls for the protection of various environmental advocacies in the Philippines. The event is part of the “Mga Kuwento ng Kasiyahan: The Grand Kapamilya Weekend,” in celebration of ABS-CBN's 60th anniversary. The five legs of One Run, One Philippines will have different environmental beneficiaries, specifically:


  • Quezon City (Kapit Bisig para sa Ilog Pasig’s Run for the Pasig River) – Rehabilitation of esteros of Manila and Quezon City
  • Cebu (RNG Kapamilya Run) – Coastal conservation in Daan Paz
  • Davao (RNG Kapamilya Run) – Ecotourism projects in Marilog Tourist Center
  • Bacolod (RNG Kapamilya Run) – Mangrove and livelihood projects in Punta Taytay, Sum-ag River rehabilitation, and ecotourism projects in Bacolod City Water District Campuestuhan Watershed
  • Burbank (TFC Global Kapamilya Run) – Ecotourism projects under the Greenfund of ABS-CBN Foundation International

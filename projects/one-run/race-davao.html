ABOUT THE KAPAMILYA RUN

The Kapamilya Run is organized by the ABS-CBN Regional News Group to celebrate the 25th anniversary of its Bacolod, Cebu and Davao stations. The runs are part of “Mga Kuwento ng Kasiyahan: The Grand Kapamilya Weekend,” an event celebrating ABS-CBN’s 60th anniversary on television. They are also in solidarity with other running events in Quezon City and Los Angeles, collectively dubbed “One Run, One Philippines.”

Proceeds from the Kapamilya Run legs in Cebu, Bacolod and Davao will be used in environmental projects adopted by ABS-CBN Lingkod Kapamilya Foundation as part of Bantay Kalikasan and RNG’s thrust to promote eco-tourism in these areas. Aside from these runs, ABS-CBN RNG has also organized “Halad Kapamilya,” a public service venue offering assistance in healthcare, government transactions and livelihood opportunities.
